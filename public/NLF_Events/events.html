Programs offered by TON

Welcome to the Temple of Nature, where we offer a diverse array of programs designed to promote community well-being by harnessing the principles of Panja Bootham (the five elements) and human health. Our holistic approach integrates ancient wisdom with modern practices to nurture harmony and balance within ourselves and the environment.

Explore our Program Offerings:

Panja Bootham Harmony Retreats:

  • Objective: Integrating all five elements for holistic well-being.
  • Activities: Comprehensive retreats combining elemental yoga, meditation, nutrition, nature, and interaction to harmonize the influence of all elements.

Prithvi (Earth) Wellness Programs:

  • Objective: Foster nourishment and vitality of body, mind, and spirit.
  • Activities: Join our nutrition workshops focusing on earthy foods, grounding practices, and outdoor activities that foster a deep connection with the Earth element. By emphasizing nutrition workshops centered around earthy foods and outdoor activities that facilitate a deeper connection with nature, these programs aim to nourish the body, mind, and spirit

Ap (Water) Health Initiatives:

  • Objective: Optimize fluid balance and detoxification, and support emotional well-being.
  • Activities: Participate in hydration workshops, learn about plant-based nutrition, and engage in purification rituals emphasizing the importance of clean and nature water consumption. These initiatives promote hydration, emotional balance, and rejuvenation.

Tejas or Agni (Fire) Wellness Workshops:

  • Objective: Strengthen digestive fire (Agni) and promote metabolic health.
  • Activities::Attend our cooking demonstrations featuring metabolism-boosting foods, participate in Agni-enhancing yoga sessions, and join workshops focused on maintaining body temperature balance. These workshops ignite the fire within, fostering vitality and transformation.

Vayu (Air) Respiratory Wellness Programs:

  • Objective: Ensure smooth airflow for respiratory and nervous system health.
  • Activities: Immerse yourself in pranayama and breathing exercises, attend joint health workshops, and experience mindfulness meditation sessions designed to balance Vayu. These programs promote respiratory health, mental clarity, and emotional equilibrium.

Akasha (Ether) Mental Clarity Initiatives:

  • Objective: Promote mental clarity, inspiration, and consciousness.
  • Activities: :Discover the power of sound therapy sessions, engage in guided visualization workshops, and immerse yourself in meditation programs aimed at connecting with higher consciousness. These initiatives foster clarity of mind, creativity, and spiritual growth.

    The vision of Org Nature Life Foundation (ONLF) is rooted in fostering a world where humanity harmonizes with the sacred elements of Panja Bootham—Air, Water, Earth, Sky, and Fire. With a profound commitment to nature-centric living.
